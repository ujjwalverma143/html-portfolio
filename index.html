Homemade Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 ¼ cups (400g) allpurpose flour
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) instant yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm milk (around 110°F/45°C)
  • 3 tbsp (45g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

For the Filling:

  • ⅔ cup (135g) brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp (15g) ground cinnamon
  • tbsp (60g) unsalted butter, softened

For the Cream Cheese Glaze:

  1. 4 oz (113g) cream cheese, softened
  2. 2 tbsp (30g) unsalted butter, softened
  3. ¾ cup (90g) powdered sugar
  4. ½ tsp vanilla extract
  5. 1–2 tbsp (15–30ml) milk (for consistency)

Instructions

Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add warm milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla extract. Mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough for 8–10 minutes on a floured surface or 5 minutes with a stand mixer until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm spot for 1–2 hours, or until doubled in size.
Make the Filling: Mix brown sugar and cinnamon in a bowl. Spread softened butter evenly over the rolledout dough.
Roll and Cut: Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a 15x10inch (38x25 cm) rectangle on a floured surface. Spread the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the buttered dough. Roll the dough tightly into a log starting from the long side. Pinch the seam to seal it. Cut the log into 12 even pieces.
Second Rise: Arrange the rolls in a greased 9x13inch (23x33 cm) baking dish, leaving space for them to expand. Cover with a towel and let rise for 30–45 minutes, or until puffed.
Bake the Rolls: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the rolls for 20–25 minutes, or until golden brown on top.
Make the Glaze: Beat cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract in a bowl. Add milk, one tablespoon at a time, until the glaze reaches your desired consistency.
Finish and Serve: Spread the glaze over warm rolls. Serve immediately and enjoy!
This recipe makes soft, gooey cinnamon rolls perfect for breakfast or a sweet treat!
